About Paws & Hearts

Dedicated to finding loving homes for pets in need since 2020

Our Mission

At Paws & Hearts, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need while educating the community about responsible pet ownership.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate animal lovers, Paws & Hearts has grown from a small local shelter to a recognized force in animal welfare. We've helped thousands of pets find their forever homes and continue to expand our reach and impact.

What We Do

  • Rescue and shelter animals in need
  • Provide medical care and rehabilitation
  • Facilitate adoptions to loving homes
  • Offer community education programs
  • Collaborate with other animal welfare organizations
